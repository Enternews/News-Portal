The Cowabunga Crew's Ultimate Reunion: Why Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: Secret of the Ooze Still Rules

ENTER News. “I'm like, totally stoked that the TMNT are back in theaters! I grew up with these radical dudes, and 'Secret of the Ooze' is still the most tubular Turtle movie out there! Can't wait to relive the nostalgia and see the newbies experience the awesomeness for themselves!”

August brought a most excellent surprise: the original Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles film got a theatrical re-release, and it was a huge hit! With a box office haul of $3.3 million, Fathom and Saga Arts decided to keep the TMNT magic alive by rereleasing the sequel, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze. Allegedly from Polygon, this 1991 classic still holds up as the most fun to be had with the Turtles on film.

Set soon after the events of the first film, Secret of the Ooze follows the Turtles as they adapt to life in the human world, crashing at April O'Neil's apartment until they find a new sewer lair. Meanwhile, the Shredder is plotting his revenge, creating two monstrous new mutants to take down our heroes. This sequel's tone is significantly lighter than its predecessor, with the Turtles cracking jokes and taking things less seriously. The result is a delightful, action-packed romp that's equal parts drama, humor, and heart.

The film's visuals are a major highlight, with impressive artistry from Jim Henson's Creature Shop bringing the new villains, Tokka and Rahzar, to life. These larger-than-life creations are not only menacing but also hilariously infantile, making them a joy to watch. And let's not forget the Shredder's epic Super Shredder transformation – a true spectacle that, while underutilized, is a testament to the creative genius behind the film.

The Secret of the Ooze is also famous for its cameo by Vanilla Ice, who performed his hit single "Ninja Rap" in the film. This scene is a nostalgic treat for fans of the 1990s rap scene and a fun nod to the era's pop culture.

In conclusion, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze is a true masterpiece that still holds up today. Its lighthearted tone, engaging story, and memorable characters make it a must-see for both nostalgic fans and new viewers alike. If you haven't experienced the awesomeness of this film yet, grab some popcorn, gather your friends, and get ready to Cowabunga with the Turtles!

"I'm totally stoked to see the TMNT back in theaters! The Secret of the Ooze is my all-time favorite Turtle movie – it's the perfect blend of action, humor, and heart. I'm counting down the days until I can relive the magic with my friends and family!"
